Wubb Idol is a made-for-television movie based on Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!.

A popular group from Wuzzlewood, the Wubb Girlz, visit Wuzzleburg by train instead of by plane. Wubbzy doesn't know the Wubb Girlz. Wubbzy shows them all around Wuzzleburg. Fans of the Wubb Girlz kept looking around Wuzzleburg for the Girlz until night.

Wubbzy was the winner of Wuzzleburg Idol Talent Show. The Wuzzleburg group had a going away party for Wubbzy. Wubbzy, Daizy, and Widget wants to stop at fun places along the way, but Walden warns them that there is no time. Wubbzy sneaks to Wacky World and have his fun. The Wubb Girlz were also there.

Wubbzy, Widget, Daizy, Walden, and the Wubb Girlz fly to Wuzzlewood on the Wubb Jet. Wubbzy gets his big makeover, and says doesn't need his best friends. Wubbzy later gets stage fright because of the large crowds ready to see his performance. Walden then attempts to hypnotize him, but the plan backfires and Walden hypnotizes himself. The Wubb Girlz told him to sing with his friends so Wubbzy, Daizy, Widget and Walden sings with the Wubb Girlz and Wubbzy doesn't feel afraid anymore.

After the concert, the Wubb Girlz start acting in their own movie, Kung Fu Princesses. After getting roped in an accident with a big avalanche of Kickety Kick Balls, the director of the movie wants Wubbzy to be a star in it - a literal star in the sky. However, despite all odds, he ends up being a huge success and starts an acting career, but soon enough Wubbzy misses his old life back in Wuzzleburg and returns to his friends once more.
